Built in 1955, this nearly 2000 square foot ranch house features 4 bedrooms and 1 full bathroom. Located in the Holland Heights neighborhood, it is only 1 block from an elementary school and church, and only a few blocks from Meijer grocery, as well as several banks and restaurants.

Oak hardwood floors embellish he living room and master bedroom, black and white tile decorates the kitchen and dining room, and sturdy laminate wood flooring shields the bathroom and hallway.

The 0.27 acre lot features a large back yard with a brick patio, garden beds, shed and ample space. The 2 car attached garage provides plenty of room for cars and storage.

Gas heat and central air keep the temperature comfortable throughout with little cost thanks to well-insulated walls and attic. On those cold winter days, the cozy fireplace will keep your outsides and insides warm.

All appliances will remain, including a dishwasher, new washer and dryer, microwave, range/oven, refrigerator and garbage disposal.

Many improvements have been made in the last 5 years, including all new vinyl windows throughout, brick patio, and updated kitchen with new counters, sink and tile.
